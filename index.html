Fig: Human Heart (NEET Level)

Definition

The human heart is a muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body and supplies oxygen and nutrients to tissues.

Parts of Human Heart

  • Right Atrium
  • Right Ventricle
  • Left Atrium
  • Left Ventricle
  • Aorta

Functions

The heart maintains blood circulation and helps in removal of waste products from the body.
